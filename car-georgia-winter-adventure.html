Georgia Winter
Adventure Car Tour

8 Days / 7 Nights ~1,200 km Toyota 4Runner 4WD Convoy Tour

Tour Overview

Georgia in winter is a completely different country to the Georgia that summer tourists see. The sulfur bathhouses of Tbilisi steam in the cold air, Gergeti Trinity Church rises above the snowline like a medieval apparition, and Gudauri transforms into one of the Caucasus' best ski resorts. This 8-day MotoRover winter convoy is the anti-peak-season adventure — fewer tourists, warmer hospitality, and landscapes that are simply unforgettable.

All driving is done in Toyota 4Runner or Mitsubishi Pajero — serious 4WD vehicles fitted with snow tyres and chains for mountain sections. Prices are significantly lower than summer, accommodation quality is identical, and the experience is entirely unique. If you've already done Georgia in summer, this will feel like a completely new country.

Tour Highlights

Gergeti Trinity Church (2,170m) — iconic in winter snow, one of the world's great winter photographs
Gudauri ski resort — Caucasus highland skiing with runs for all levels
Tbilisi sulfur baths — steaming mineral pools in winter cold, ancient and restorative
Prometheus Cave — vast illuminated cave system, spectacular year-round
UNESCO Gelati Monastery — 12th-century golden mosaics, winter stillness
4WD winter driving — snow chains, mountain passes, dramatic highland scenery
Batumi Black Sea coast — subtropical micro-climate, mild and beautiful even in January
Georgian wine & chacha — warming winter hospitality at every table

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day Date Route Activity Meals Distance
Day 1Jan/Feb TBATbilisiArrival. Old Town snowy streets, Narikala fortress views over the mist-hung city, famous sulfur bathhouses of Abanotubani steaming in the winter air. Welcome dinner in a traditional wine cellar. Meals included — Dinner — km
Day 2—Tbilisi → GudauriDrive the Georgian Military Highway in winter conditions — a dramatic corridor of frozen gorges and snow-capped ridgelines. Arrive at Gudauri ski resort. Optional skiing and snowboarding on Caucasus slopes this afternoon. Meals included — Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner 120 km
Day 3—Gudauri → StepantsmindaThe day the itinerary was built around. Drive to Kazbegi village and hike or 4WD to Gergeti Trinity Church at 2,170m — suspended above the snowline with Mt. Kazbek behind it. One of the world's iconic winter photographs, and you are in it. Meals included — Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner 40 km
Day 4—Stepantsminda → Gori → KutaisiDescend the Military Highway, stopping at the Stalin Museum in Gori for a fascinating slice of Soviet history. Continue west to Kutaisi, Georgia's second city and ancient capital, through wide winter valleys. Meals included — Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner 300 km
Day 5—KutaisiMorning visit to UNESCO Gelati Monastery — the 12th-century crown jewel of Georgian Christendom, its golden mosaics luminous in winter light. Afternoon: the extraordinary Prometheus Cave, its vast illuminated chambers even more atmospheric in winter stillness. Local feast. Meals included — Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner 60 km
Day 6—Kutaisi → BorjomiDrive to Borjomi, the famous mineral spa resort town nestled in the Borjomi Gorge. Sample the legendary mineral water at source. Winter walk through the snow-dusted Borjomi-Kharagauli National Park — Georgia's largest protected wilderness. Meals included — Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner 100 km
Day 7—Borjomi → BatumiThe Black Sea coast run. Batumi sits in a subtropical micro-climate where even January is mild and green. Arrive on the boulevard, explore the Botanical Garden's winter colours, and celebrate the journey with a farewell dinner by the sea. Meals included — Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner 200 km
Day 8—BatumiFarewell breakfast on the Black Sea promenade. Time to walk the waterfront and collect final memories before airport transfers. Departure from Batumi. Meals included — Breakfast — km
