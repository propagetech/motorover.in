Car Tour Georgia Jun 6–13, 2026

Georgia
Self-Drive

8 Days / 7 Nights ~1,500 km Toyota 4Runner / Jeep Renegade Tbilisi → Batumi

Tour Overview

Georgia is one of those rare places where antiquity and wilderness arrive together. Driving from Tbilisi's sulfur-scented old quarter through the mythical highlands of Svaneti — a landscape unchanged since the Middle Ages — to the sunlit promenades of Batumi on the Black Sea coast is not just a road trip. It is a passage through time.

This 8-day self-drive takes you behind the wheel of a Toyota 4Runner or Jeep Renegade across mountain highways, unpaved Svaneti tracks, and coastal roads. You'll navigate cave cities carved into cliff faces, cross high Caucasian passes, share a traditional supra feast with locals, and end your journey with your feet in the Black Sea. Georgia is Europe's most underrated road trip country — and this tour proves it.

Tour Highlights

UNESCO Mtskheta — Georgia's ancient spiritual capital and oldest living city
Vardzia cave monastery — 12th-century rock-cut city commissioned by Queen Tamar
Mythical Svaneti region with UNESCO-listed medieval defensive towers
Georgian Military Highway — one of the most dramatic mountain drives in Europe
Black Sea arrival at Batumi — beaches, botanical gardens, and boulevard nights
Traditional supra feast with wine, chacha, and Georgian polyphonic song
Ancient Uplistsikhe cave city — pagan rock-cut town dating to the 6th century BC
Wine and chacha tasting in the world's oldest wine-producing country

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day Date Route Activity Distance
Day 1 Jun 6 Tbilisi Arrival. Old City Tbilisi, Narikala Fortress, the famous sulfur bathhouses of Abanotubani, welcome dinner in a traditional wine cellar. — km
Day 2 Jun 7 Tbilisi → Mtskheta → Gori → Uplistsikhe Visit UNESCO-listed Mtskheta, Georgia's ancient capital and cradle of Christianity. Drive to Gori, then explore Uplistsikhe — a hauntingly beautiful pagan rock-cut cave city above the Mtkvari River. 150 km
Day 3 Jun 8 Gori → Borjomi → Akhaltsikhe → Vardzia Descend into the mineral springs spa valley of Borjomi. Tour the fairy-tale Rabati Castle in Akhaltsikhe. Arrive at Vardzia — a 12th-century cave monastery city carved into a volcanic cliff face by Queen Tamar. 180 km
Day 4 Jun 9 Vardzia → Mestia (Svaneti) The most dramatic drive of the tour. Climb into the remote highlands of Svaneti — a region so isolated it retained its medieval identity intact. Arrive in Mestia, surrounded by Ushba glacier peaks and ancient koshki towers. 270 km
Day 5 Jun 10 Mestia Rest Day Hike toward the Ushba glacier for sweeping Caucasus panoramas. Explore the UNESCO-listed Svan defensive towers. Evening: a full traditional supra feast — polyphonic toasts, homebrade wine, and the unforgettable Georgian table. — km
Day 6 Jun 11 Mestia → Zugdidi → Batumi Descend from the Caucasus highlands through lush Samegrelo lowlands to Zugdidi, then onward to the Black Sea coast. Batumi arrival — the jewel of Georgia's coastline. 230 km
Day 7 Jun 12 Batumi Explore the famous Batumi Botanical Garden, relax at Black Sea beaches, stroll the art-deco Batumi Boulevard, and take in the city's famous night skyline. Farewell dinner by the sea. — km
Day 8 Jun 13 Batumi Departure. Airport transfers included. Take a piece of Georgia home with you. — km
