The Silk Road
Drive

8 Days / 7 Nights ~1,700 km Toyota Sequoia Convoy Tour

Tour Overview

Kyrgyzstan — one of the best-kept secrets of Central Asia. This self-drive expedition puts you behind the wheel of a Toyota Sequoia as you navigate snow-capped mountains, glacial rivers, turquoise alpine lakes, deep gorges, and endless grasslands along the ancient Silk Road.

Drive in a guided convoy with a lead car, sweep car, and a mechanic on standby. Experience traditional horse games, eagle hunting demonstrations, and nights in authentic yurt camps under a sky unpolluted by city light. This is road tripping as it was meant to be.

Tour Highlights

Drive alongside turquoise Issyk-Kul lake — one of the world's largest alpine lakes
Navigate dramatic mountain passes and high-altitude plateaus
Watch traditional Kyrgyz horse games & eagle hunting
Overnight stay at Song-Kul — a 3,016m alpine yurt camp
Mix of smooth highways & unpaved Silk Route mountain tracks
Grigorievka Gorge day trip — glacial streams & walnut forests
Toyota Sequoia automatic — powerful and comfortable on any terrain
Lead car + sweep car + mechanic + medical support
