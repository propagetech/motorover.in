Gear & Equipment

Yes — riding gear and helmet are your responsibility and must be brought from India. This includes jacket, pants, gloves, boots, and a certified helmet. Protective gear is mandatory on all motorcycle tours — no exceptions. This is for your safety.
Yes. A dedicated luggage van travels with all motorcycle tours, so you only carry a small day bag on the bike. For car tours, luggage travels in your vehicle.
Every group tour includes a mechanic and support vehicle. Minor repairs are handled on the road. For major issues, a replacement motorcycle will be arranged. Breakdown assistance is fully included in the tour price.

Visa & Travel

Indian passport holders require an e-visa for Kyrgyzstan, which costs approximately INR 5,000. We provide a visa support letter to assist your application. The visa is not included in the tour price.
Travel insurance is strongly recommended and is your responsibility. A good policy covering adventure activities, medical evacuation, and trip cancellation costs approximately INR 2,000–3,000. We can suggest coverage types but do not arrange it on your behalf.
Yes — for both motorcycle and car tours, an International Driving Permit (IDP) is required in most destinations. Obtain this from your local RTO before departure. Cost and processing time vary by state.
No — international flights are not included. We provide the start and end city of each tour, and you book your own flights. We recommend specific flight windows and routes during the booking process. For reference, flights to Bishkek typically cost INR 45,000–50,000.

Cancellation Policy

Our detailed cancellation and refund policy is available at the booking stage and on our full Terms & Conditions page. In general, cancellations made well in advance receive a higher refund. If we cancel a tour due to circumstances within our control, a full refund or credit towards a future tour will be offered. For force majeure events (natural disasters, political unrest, pandemics), our force majeure policy applies — full details in the Terms & Conditions.
