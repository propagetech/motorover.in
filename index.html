Why Choose Us

The MotoRover Difference

Every decision we make is built around one thing — giving you the best possible adventure experience.

Arrive & Ride

Step off the plane and straight onto your adventure. Motorcycles, SUVs, accommodation, meals — everything is pre-arranged and personally vetted by our founder.

Safety First

All routes are thoroughly scouted before launch. Protective gear is mandatory. Support vehicles, a mechanic, and a first-aid kit accompany every group tour.

Expert Leadership

Tours are led by founder Piyush Sonsale — former motorcycle performance tester & auto journalist with 6 years at Bike India and Car India.

Cultural Immersion

Routes traverse off-the-beaten-track landscapes emphasising local culture, regional cuisine, traditional horse games, eagle hunting, and nomadic experiences.

Community of Riders

Meet fellow adventure lovers from India and around the world. Our tours build friendships that last well beyond the final day of riding.

Open to All Levels

Amateur or experienced, young or seasoned — our tours welcome all riders and drivers. Only requirement: a valid international licence and the spirit of adventure.
