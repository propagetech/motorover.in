Balkan
Motorcycle Tour

10 Days / 9 Nights ~1,800 km BMW / Honda / Suzuki Small Group Tour

Tour Overview

The Balkans are Europe's most compelling secret — a mosaic of ancient civilisations, turquoise coastlines, and mountain landscapes that few riders have yet discovered. This 10-day journey threads through four extraordinary countries, from the Dalmatian Coast's shimmering Adriatic waters to the raw, primeval canyons of Montenegro's interior.

Ride into Mostar across its famous Ottoman bridge, wind through Sarajevo's layers of history, and drop down to the UNESCO-listed Bay of Kotor before finishing in the walled splendour of Dubrovnik. Spring riding conditions, boutique accommodation, and a carefully curated cultural itinerary make this one of MotoRover's most beloved tours.

Tour Highlights

UNESCO Bay of Kotor — Montenegro's crown jewel
Stari Most (Old Bridge) — Mostar's iconic arch
Dubrovnik Old City walls & sunset ride
Krka National Park waterfalls & swimming
Sutjeska National Park — Bosnia's primeval forest
Adriatic coastal roads along the Dalmatian Coast
4 countries: Croatia, Bosnia, Montenegro & beyond
Spring warmth — ideal riding conditions in April
