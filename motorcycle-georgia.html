Georgia
Motorcycle Tour

8 Days / 7 Nights ~1,400 km Honda Africa Twin / Transalp 750 Group Tour

Tour Overview

Georgia is one of the most rewarding motorcycle destinations in the world — and one of the least explored by Indian riders. The Caucasus country packs millennia of history, dramatic mountain roads, 12th-century cave monasteries, a Black Sea coastline and some of the world's oldest wine traditions into a compact, welcoming landscape that costs a fraction of Western Europe.

This Tbilisi-to-Batumi route is the perfect introduction to international motorcycle touring. Roads are well-maintained, signage is clear, and the Georgian people are extraordinarily hospitable. June brings warm days, cool mountain air at altitude and long evenings on the Black Sea Riviera. With a Honda Africa Twin or Transalp 750 beneath you, the Georgian Military Highway — one of the great mountain roads of the Caucasus — is yours to conquer. First international tour? Georgia is where to start.

Tour Highlights

Gergeti Trinity Church at 2,170m — Georgia's most iconic view
Georgian Military Highway — the legendary Caucasus mountain road
Vardzia cave monastery — 12th-century rock-hewn city
Stalin Museum in Gori — history's most complex birthplace
Batumi Black Sea Riviera — subtropical coast at journey's end
Wine tasting in Tbilisi — Georgia invented wine 8,000 years ago
Ancient monasteries, fortresses and cave cities throughout
Outstanding value — Georgia is exceptionally affordable
