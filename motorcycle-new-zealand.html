New Zealand
Motorcycle Tour

9 Days / 8 Nights ~2,500 km BMW F750 GS / Triumph Tiger 660 Group Tour

Tour Overview

New Zealand's South Island is motorcycling's finest canvas — a compact, road-perfect landmass where ancient glaciers calve into the Tasman Sea, fjords split sheer mountain walls, and the highest sealed road in the country crests a panorama that stops riders in their tracks. This 9-day loop from Christchurch delivers every superlative the country has to offer.

February and March are peak summer in the Southern Hemisphere — long golden days, dry alpine passes and the Milford Road at its most spectacular. The Honda CB500X, Triumph Tiger 660 and BMW F750 GS have been chosen specifically for these roads: agile enough for the winding Haast Pass gorge, comfortable enough for 300 km days, and sure-footed on the occasional gravel detour. New Zealand is an ideal first international motorcycle tour — left-hand traffic, world-class road surfaces and infrastructure — and a bucket-list destination for seasoned riders alike.

Tour Highlights

Milford Sound — the 8th Wonder of the World, by motorcycle
Franz Josef & Fox Glaciers — ice on the edge of rainforest
Crown Range Road — New Zealand's highest sealed pass (1,076m)
Haast Pass — UNESCO World Heritage rainforest gorge
Queenstown — the adventure capital of the world
Arthur's Pass — Trans-Alpine Southern Alps crossing
