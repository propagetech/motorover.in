Northern Europe
Motorcycle Tour

8 Days / 7 Nights ~1,600 km Yamaha MT-09 / BMW R1300 GS Group Tour

Tour Overview

Three countries, three UNESCO-listed capital old towns, one Baltic coastline — and the kind of wide, forested roads that make a Yamaha MT-09 or BMW R1300 GS feel like they were built specifically for this journey. Northern Europe's Baltic States are among the most underrated motorcycle destinations in the world: cultured, affordable, and utterly free of tourist crowds.

August in the Baltics means long evenings that barely darken, perfect road temperatures and the quiet satisfaction of riding between medieval cities that most of the world hasn't discovered yet. Tallinn's perfectly preserved old town gives way to Jurmala's pine-fringed Soviet beach resort, then Riga's extraordinary Art Nouveau streetscapes, the forested gorges of Gauja National Park, the surreal Hill of Crosses in Lithuania, and finally the Baroque grandeur of Vilnius. This is Europe at its most unhurried — and most rewarding.

Tour Highlights

UNESCO Tallinn Old Town — medieval towers and cobblestones
Riga Art Nouveau — the world's finest collection on two wheels
Gauja National Park gorge — Latvia's most dramatic landscape
Baltic Sea coast — Cape Kolka, dunes and a Soviet beach resort
Hill of Crosses, Šiauliai — one of the world's great pilgrim sites
UNESCO Vilnius Baroque old city — the hidden gem of the Baltics
3 countries in 8 days — Estonia, Latvia & Lithuania
Midsummer August light — evenings that last until midnight
