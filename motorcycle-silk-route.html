Silk Route
Motorcycle Tour

7 Days / 6 Nights ~1,300 km Honda CRF 250 Group Tour

Tour Overview

Kyrgyzstan is a magical land of snowcapped mountains and turquoise alpine lakes — one of Central Asia's best-kept secrets. This guided motorcycle adventure explores the ancient Silk Route, combining paved mountain roads with off-road trails through high passes and vast grasslands.

Cultural highlights include traditional horse games, eagle hunting demonstrations, yurt lunches with nomadic families, and folklore performances that offer rare insight into Kyrgyz nomadic life. With a Honda CRF 250 beneath you and the Tian Shan mountains around you, this is motorcycling at its most pure.
