Motorcycle Tour South Africa Oct 27 – Nov 4, 2025

South Africa
Motorcycle Tour

9 Days / 8 Nights ~2,200 km BMW GS Series Small Group Tour

Tour Overview

South Africa is a country that rewards the motorcyclist like no other — a vast, wild nation where every landscape is cinematic, every pass is a masterpiece, and every stretch of road opens onto something extraordinary. This nine-day circuit from Cape Town is among the finest riding itineraries on the African continent, taking in the entire sweep of the Western Cape.

From the vertiginous cliffs of Chapman's Peak Drive to the towering forest canopy of Tsitsikamma, the moonscape semi-desert of the Great Karoo, and the legendary Swartberg Pass — one of Africa's most celebrated mountain roads — this tour covers ~2,200 km of riding that will stay with you long after you've touched down back home. October–November brings warm spring weather, wildflowers in the Western Cape, and whale-watching along the Hermanus coast.

Tour Highlights

Chapman's Peak Drive — one of the world's great coastal roads
Cape of Good Hope — Africa's iconic southern tip
Garden Route — coastal forests, lagoons & the Knysna Heads
Swartberg Pass (2,200m) — Africa's most spectacular gravel pass
Great Karoo — vast semi-desert, Valley of Desolation
Whale-watching coast at Hermanus in spring season
Boulders Beach African penguin colony — unforgettable
BMW GS with full guide, mechanic & support vehicle

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day Date Route Activity Distance
Day 1 Oct 27 Cape Town Arrival at Cape Town International. Bike handover, Table Mountain cable car, welcome dinner with views over the Atlantic. — km
Day 2 Oct 28 Cape Peninsula Loop Chapman's Peak Drive, Cape of Good Hope Nature Reserve, Boulder's Beach penguin colony — a full day on Africa's most dramatic peninsula. 180 km
Day 3 Oct 29 Cape Town → Swellendam Hermanus whale-watching coast, the Overberg passes, and picturesque Swellendam — one of South Africa's oldest towns. 280 km
Day 4 Oct 30 Swellendam → George Begin the Garden Route proper — Mossel Bay, the Wilderness lagoon, the Outeniqua Pass, arriving in George with ocean views throughout. 250 km
Day 5 Oct 31 George → Tsitsikamma Outeniqua Pass, Knysna Heads sea cliffs, the ancient yellowwood forests of Tsitsikamma — South Africa's Garden Route at its wildest. 220 km
Day 6 Nov 1 Tsitsikamma → Port Elizabeth Storms River Gorge suspension bridge, Bloukrans Bridge (world's highest commercial bungee jump — optional). Arrive Port Elizabeth (Gqeberha). 200 km
Day 7 Nov 2 Port Elizabeth → Graaff-Reinet Leave the coast behind and enter the vast Great Karoo semi-desert. Valley of Desolation, ancient dolerite columns, and the jewel-like Graaff-Reinet. 350 km
Day 8 Nov 3 Karoo → Cape Town The grand finale — the legendary Swartberg Pass (2,200m, unpaved). Descend through Prince Albert, the winelands of Paarl, and back to Cape Town. 450 km
