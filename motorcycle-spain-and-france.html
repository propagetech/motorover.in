Spain & France
Motorcycle Tour

8 Days / 7 Nights ~1,200 km BMW GS Series Small Group Tour

Tour Overview

Europe's most captivating riding corridor awaits — eight days of pure motorcycle bliss through two of the continent's most iconic countries. From the golden streets of Barcelona to the soaring Pyrenean passes shared by Tour de France legends, this tour delivers a perfect blend of Mediterranean warmth, alpine drama, and cultural richness.

Aboard a BMW GS adventure machine, you'll carve through the medieval lanes of Girona, hug the clifftop roads of the Costa Brava, climb into Andorra at 2,408 metres, and descend through the French Pyrenees on the very cols that define cycling mythology. Small group, expert guide, and flawless logistics — this is European motorcycling at its finest.

Tour Highlights

Costa Brava coastal roads above the Mediterranean
Pyrenees alpine passes soaring to 2,400m+
Andorra — Col d'Envalira at 2,408m highest in Pyrenees
Iconic Tour de France cols — Col du Tourmalet & more
Medieval Girona old town, cobbled streets & cathedral
Val d'Aran — the Pyrenees' hidden Gascon valley
BMW F800 / F900 / R1250 / R1300 GS — your choice
Small group: guide, mechanic, support vehicle
