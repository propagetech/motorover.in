Ultimate Alps
Motorcycle Tour

9 Days / 8 Nights ~2,000 km BMW R1250/R1300 GS Group Tour

Tour Overview

The Ultimate Alps is the bucket-list motorcycle journey. Four countries in nine days — Germany, Austria, Italy, and Switzerland — traversing the greatest mountain roads in Europe on a BMW R1250 GS or R1300 GS, machines literally built for this terrain.

This is not just a tour; it's a greatest-hits album of European alpine motorcycling. Stelvio Pass (2,757m, 48 hairpin bends — the world's most famous mountain road), the UNESCO Dolomites (a cathedral of orange rock towers), Furka Pass (made famous by James Bond's Goldfinger), Swiss Engadin valley, Great St. Bernard Pass, and views of Mont Blanc from Chamonix. Every day brings a legendary road.
