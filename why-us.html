The MotoRover Difference

Why Choose Us

There are other adventure travel companies. Here's why 1,000+ Indian travellers have chosen MotoRover — and why they keep coming back.

01

Arrive & Ride

Step off the plane and straight onto your adventure. Every single element of your tour is pre-arranged, personally vetted, and ready to go — no logistics stress, no surprises.

  • Motorcycle or car waiting for you at the airport
  • All accommodation booked & inspected in advance
  • Every meal arranged — local & delicious
  • Every route personally scouted by Piyush before launch
02

Safety First

Adventure doesn't mean reckless. Our safety systems are built from over a decade of international touring experience — so you can push limits with confidence.

  • Protective gear mandatory on all motorcycle tours
  • Dedicated mechanic on every group tour
  • Support/sweep vehicle with first-aid kit
  • 24/7 remote support for independent travellers
  • All destinations thoroughly researched & scouted
03

Expert Leadership

You're not being guided by a travel agent who has read a brochure. You're riding with a former motorcycle performance tester and auto journalist who has ridden the world's greatest roads professionally.

  • Founder Piyush Sonsale leads or personally supervises every tour
  • 6 years at Bike India & Car India as a performance tester
  • National Motorcycle Racing Championship competitor
  • Local guides with deep destination knowledge
04

Real Cultural Immersion

We don't just take you through beautiful places — we take you into them. Off-the-beaten-track routes, authentic local stays, and cultural experiences you won't find in any package tour catalogue.

  • Overnight yurt camps with nomadic Kyrgyz families
  • Traditional horse games & eagle hunting displays
  • Regional cuisine at every meal — no tourist menus
  • Routes that avoid highways in favour of scenic back roads
05

Open to All Levels

You don't need to be an experienced international rider to join us. We've taken first-timers on international rides, 60-year-olds on mountain trails, and non-riders on car tours. One requirement: the spirit of adventure.

  • Amateur to experienced — all riding levels welcomed
  • Age & gender are no barriers
  • Car tours ideal for non-riders who love road trips
  • Only requirement: valid international driving licence
